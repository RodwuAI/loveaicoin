## 8. 关于页面文案 (About Page)

### 项目故事 (3段)

#### 第一段：缘起
**中文**:  
2023年，当我们第一次让ChatGPT帮我们写代码、做翻译、生成创意时，我们意识到：AI不是未来的事，它就在今天。但我们也看到，大多数人站在AI的门外——好奇但不知从何开始，担忧但不知道如何应对。

**English**:  
In 2023, when we first used ChatGPT to write code, translate documents, and spark creative ideas, we realized: AI isn't the future. It's today. Yet we also saw that most people stood outside AI's door—curious but unsure where to start, concerned but uncertain how to respond.

#### 第二段：使命
**中文**:  
LAC诞生于一个简单的问题：如何让更多人真正用起来？不是看热闹，不是焦虑观望，而是动手学习、实际应用、创造价值。我们相信，当学习AI能获得即时回报，当使用AI能创造真实价值，拥抱变化就不再困难。

**English**:  
LAC was born from a simple question: How do we get more people to actually use AI? Not as spectators, not from anxious sidelines, but by hands-on learning, practical application, and value creation. We believe that when learning AI brings immediate rewards and using AI creates real value, embracing change becomes effortless.

#### 第三段：现在
**中文**:  
今天，LAC是一个由Solana驱动的去中心化激励网络。在这里，每一个认真学习AI的人都被看见，每一个用AI解决问题的人都被奖励，每一个帮助他人的贡献者都被尊重。这不是乌托邦，这是正在发生的现实。

**English**:  
Today, LAC is a Solana-powered decentralized incentive network. Here, every person seriously learning AI is seen. Everyone solving problems with AI is rewarded. Every contributor helping others is respected. This isn't utopia. This is happening now.
