# TimeRedeem Partner Tier Structure

## Overview

TimeRedeem operates a three-tier partner ecosystem designed to incentivize participation at different levels of commitment and contribution.

## Partner Tiers

### Tier 1: Time Opener (时间启幕者)

**Description**: Early adopters and ecosystem builders who join in the initial phase.

**Entry Requirements**:
- Participate in ecosystem construction
- Hold 0.1% ecosystem share
- Complete onboarding process

**Benefits**:
- Priority access to time asset issuance rights
- Ecosystem governance voting rights (0.1% weight)
- Early project participation qualification
- Exclusive metaverse identity badge
- Direct access to core team
- Quarterly strategy meetings

**Obligations**:
- Actively participate in ecosystem promotion
- Maintain TimeRedeem brand image
- Participate in quarterly ecosystem meetings
- Provide feedback on product development

**Reward Structure**:
- Base ecosystem share: 0.1%
- Revenue sharing from ecosystem growth
- Priority allocation in new initiatives

---

### Tier 2: Consensus Node Partner (共识节点合伙人)

**Description**: Full partners who operate consensus nodes and maintain network security.

**Entry Requirements**:
- Operate a consensus node (24/7 online)
- Technical capability to maintain node infrastructure
- Minimum stake requirement (variable by region)
- KYC verification completed

**Benefits**:
- Consensus node reward distribution
- Full ecosystem governance voting rights
- Transaction fee sharing
- Priority access to AI twin technology
- Priority metaverse land subscription rights
- Dedicated technical support
- Monthly performance reports
- Annual partner summit invitation

**Obligations**:
- Maintain node 24/7 uptime (99.9% SLA)
- Participate in network consensus validation
- Refer at least 3 sub-nodes within 6 months
- Submit quarterly performance reports
- Participate in network upgrades
- Maintain security standards

**Reward Structure**:
- Base consensus reward: $500/month
- Direct referral bonus: 20% of referred node revenue
- Transaction fee share: Proportional to node stake
- Performance bonuses for uptime > 99.9%

**Node Requirements**:
- Minimum hardware specs: 8 CPU, 32GB RAM, 500GB SSD
- Network: 100Mbps dedicated bandwidth
- Location: Distributed across regions for decentralization

---

### Tier 3: Referral Node Partner (推荐节点合伙人)

**Description**: Partners focused on network expansion and new partner acquisition.

**Entry Requirements**:
- Demonstrated network building capability
- Community influence or existing network
- Completion of referral training program

**Benefits**:
- Direct referral reward: 20% of referred partner's first-year revenue
- Indirect referral reward: 10% (Level 2), 5% (Level 3)
- Team performance sharing
- Training and marketing support
- Co-branded marketing materials
- Monthly team leader calls
- Fast-track support channel

**Obligations**:
- Monthly referral targets (minimum 2 qualified leads/month)
- Assist with new partner onboarding
- Maintain partner relationship quality
- Attend monthly training sessions
- Report on network health

**Reward Structure**:
- Direct Referral: 20% commission
- Level 2 Referral: 10% commission
- Level 3 Referral: 5% commission
- Team Volume Bonus: Additional 5% for teams > 10 active partners
- Quarterly performance bonuses

**Promotion Path**:
- Top referral partners can upgrade to Consensus Node status
- Requires 20+ active direct referrals and $100K+ team volume

## Tier Comparison Matrix

| Feature | Time Opener | Consensus Node | Referral Node |
|---------|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| Entry Cost | Low | Medium-High | Low |
| Time Commitment | Low | High | Medium |
| Technical Skill | None | Required | None |
| Revenue Potential | Medium | High | Very High |
| Governance Rights | Limited (0.1%) | Full | None |
| Network Building | Optional | Required | Primary Focus |
| Support Level | Standard | Premium | Enhanced |

## Upgrade Paths

### Time Opener → Consensus Node
- Requirements: 6 months as Opener, demonstrate commitment, pass technical assessment
- Benefits: Increased revenue share, full governance rights

### Referral Node → Consensus Node
- Requirements: 20+ direct referrals, $100K team volume, technical readiness
- Benefits: Stable base income + referral income

### Consensus Node → Super Node
- Requirements: 100+ nodes in network, $1M+ transaction volume
- Benefits: Regional leadership, additional revenue share, board representation

## Governance Structure

Voting power distribution:
- Time Openers: 0.1% per share (capped at 5% per individual)
- Consensus Nodes: Proportional to stake and uptime
- Super Nodes: Fixed 10% voting power each (max 5 super nodes)

## Performance Metrics

All partners tracked on:
- Activity score
- Contribution index
- Network growth attributed
- Community engagement
- Governance participation

Top performers receive:
- Annual awards
- Additional allocation in new projects
- Exclusive event invitations
- Advisory board consideration
