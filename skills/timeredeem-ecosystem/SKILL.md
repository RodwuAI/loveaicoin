## Partner Onboarding Workflow

1. **Initial Assessment**
   - Determine partner tier (时间启幕者, 共识节点, etc.)
   - Review partner background and network
   - Assess contribution potential

2. **Agreement Generation**
   - Use `scripts/generate_partner_agreement.py`
   - Customize terms based on tier
   - Include specific reward structures

3. **Document Package**
   - Partnership agreement
   - Time生态总纲 (Ecosystem outline)
   - Onboarding checklist
   - Brand assets and guidelines

4. **Integration**
   - Add to partner tracking system
   - Schedule onboarding call
   - Assign buddy/partner mentor

## Consensus Node Management

### Node Tiers
- **时间启幕者 (Time Opener)**: Early adopters, 0.1% ecosystem share
- **共识节点 (Consensus Node)**: Full nodes with voting rights
- **推荐节点 (Referral Node)**: Focus on network expansion

### Reward Calculation
Refer to `scripts/calculate_node_rewards.py` for automated calculations based on:
- Direct referrals
- Network depth
- Time asset transactions
- AI分身 performance metrics
